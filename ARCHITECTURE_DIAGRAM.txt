                              DATA FLOW EXAMPLE
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

    Starting a Game with Role Assignment:

    1. User selects "Movies" category in CategoryScreen
           ↓
    2. UI calls: GameEngine().assignRoles(players, items, hints)
           ↓
    3. Domain randomly selects 1 SPY and assigns word "Titanic" to others
           ↓
    4. Returns: List<GamePlayer> with roles
           ↓
    5. UI navigates to GameScreen
           ↓
    6. GameScreen shows each player's role one-by-one
           ↓
    7. After all players see roles → navigate to TimerScreen
           ↓
    8. TimerScreen uses: TimerManager().startCountdown(600)
           ↓
    9. Timer counts down, UI shows time
           ↓
   10. On finish: VibrationHelper.vibratePattern(context)
           ↓
   11. Navigate to VotingScreen
           ↓
   12. Players vote, UI calls: GameEngine().calculateVotingResults()
           ↓
   13. Show results: SPY caught or not
